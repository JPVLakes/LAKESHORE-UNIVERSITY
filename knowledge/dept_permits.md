## Processos

### Processo 1: Solicitacao de Documentos para Permit (Application)

- **Objetivo:** Reunir todos os documentos tecnicos necessarios para aplicar o permit no condado
- **Responsavel principal:** Kaio Vinicius
- **Frequencia:** A cada novo projeto (apos first draw para spec homes)
- **Ferramentas:** E-mail compartilhado de permit, Planilha de Permit, Builder Trend

**Passo a passo:**
1. Receber notificacao de first draw (nova casa aparece na tabela) -- Responsavel: Kaio -- Ferramenta: Planilha de Permit
2. Identificar numero da casa, endereco, condado e pasta -- Responsavel: Kaio -- Ferramenta: Planilha de Permit
3. Solicitar **Survey** ao surveyor (Anel ou Kaio) por e-mail com dados do projeto (endereco, parcel ID) -- Responsavel: Kaio -- Ferramenta: E-mail
4. Receber survey (documento base para demais solicitacoes) -- Responsavel: Kaio -- Ferramenta: E-mail
5. Solicitar **Energy Calculations** ao sub (prazo medio: mesmo dia) -- Responsavel: Kaio -- Ferramenta: E-mail
6. Solicitar **Floor Plan** ao sub (prazo medio: 5 dias) -- Responsavel: Kaio -- Ferramenta: E-mail
7. Solicitar **Septic** (prazo medio: 7 dias) -- Responsavel: Kaio -- Ferramenta: E-mail
8. Solicitar **Trusses** (usando arquivo modelo/piloto sem endereco) -- Responsavel: Kaio -- Ferramenta: E-mail
9. Marcar status de cada documento: vermelho = solicitado, verde = recebido -- Responsavel: Kaio -- Ferramenta: Planilha de Permit
10. Todos documentos recebidos: casa marcada em verde, pronta para aplicar -- Responsavel: Kaio -- Ferramenta: Planilha de Permit

**Inputs:** Notificacao de first draw, dados do projeto (endereco, parcel ID, modelo, condado)
**Outputs:** Pasta completa de documentos (Survey, Energy Calculations, Floor Plan, Septic, Trusses)
**Observacoes:** Para spec homes, stages de application sao SEMPRE os mesmos: survey, floor plan, energy calculations, septic, trusses. Survey e prerequisito obrigatorio para floor plan, energy e septic. O documento de survey deve ser anexado automaticamente as solicitacoes subsequentes.

## Dores e Problemas Conhecidos

- **Nao recebem e-mail de aviso de expiracao de permit** (Marion County) - risco de cancelamento de permit
- Gestao de modifications e extensions e completamente manual
- 16+ POs/daily logs duplicados para mesmo dono em condominios
- Comunicacao de hold nao e centralizada para toda empresa
- Documentos com versoes erradas podem chegar ao field (sem controle de versionamento)
- Historico de projeto escrito manualmente - gasta muito tempo para reproduzir
- Informacao repetida constantemente para Client Relations (status que ja esta na planilha)
- Comunicacao manual com Dandara (Utilities) sobre septic permit
- Processo manual para casas customizadas sem padronizacao
- Waivers e Arrows em Citrus County geram retrabalho recorrente
- Falta de sistema de busca por qualquer atributo (nome do dono, parcel ID, permit number)

## Key Glossary Terms

> Terms from [Glossario de Termos](03_GLOSSARIO.md) most relevant to the Permits department.

- **Permit** / **Licenca de Construcao/Alvara**: County-issued license authorizing construction start -- process varies by county
- **Survey** / **Levantamento Topografico**: Land survey by licensed professional -- first document requested, base for all others
- **NOC (Notice of Commencement)** / **Notificacao de Inicio de Obra**: Formal notice of construction start -- requires client signature before work begins
- **Floor Plan** / **Planta Baixa**: Primary project document feeding the entire construction process -- key for permit application
- **Impact Fee** / **Taxa de Impacto**: County fee for infrastructure impact -- credit possible for demolition of existing structure
- **Builders Risk** / **Seguro de Construcao**: Construction insurance acquired at start -- can only be filed after permit payment
- **Waiver** / **Dispensa/Isencao**: Formal exemption from county requirement (e.g., lot height below minimum) -- ~40% of Citrus County homes
- **HOA (Home Owners Association)** / **Associacao de Proprietarios**: May require additional approvals before construction in some areas
- **Site Plan** / **Plano do Terreno**: Plan showing house location on lot, access, utilities -- sent to sub for clear lot
- **Setback** / **Recuo**: Minimum distance between structure and property boundary required by zoning code
- **Construction Charges** / **Taxas de Construcao**: Fees paid to county or utility company during construction -- usually within first draw
- **Septic** / **Sistema Septico**: Private sewage treatment system -- more common in Marion County, requires DOH inspection

## Permit Process Detail (Batch 3)

### County-Specific Permit Submission Requirements (from 5 Permit Validation interviews)

Each county has distinct requirements for permit submission. From Batch 3 interviews with Kaio, Cassia, and Dandara:

**All Counties Require:**
- Floor Plan, Energy Calculations, Septic Document, Trusses, Survey, NOC, Product Approval List

**County-Specific Additional Requirements:**
| County | Additional Documents | Portal | Notes |
|--------|---------------------|--------|-------|
| Marion County | Application Form | Accela (aca-prod.accela.com/MARION/) | No expiration email warnings -- team discovers expiration "by surprise" |
| Citrus County | Application Forms, Civic Application | Accela (aca3.accela.com/CitrusCounty/) | ~40% of houses need Waiver (lot height); Arrows (drainage signage) recurrent |
| Sarasota County | Drainage Plan, Application Form | TBD | Additional drainage documentation required |
| Charlotte County | TBD | charlottecountyfl.gov | Planned for future expansion |

**Spec Homes Application Stages (always the same):**
1. Survey (prerequisite for all other documents)
2. Floor Plan (from architect, different from survey)
3. Energy Calculations (same-day turnaround typical)
4. Septic (7-day typical turnaround)
5. Trusses (uses pilot/model file without address/parcel ID)

**Custom Homes Additional Complexity:**
- No standardized stages -- each project may require unique documents
- Examples: wetland determination, HOA approval, demolition permit, NOC with client signature
- Pre-agreement status (authorized by Bernardo to work without fixed contract)
- Knowledge concentrated in Kaio and Cassia -- risk if either leaves

### Timeline Expectations by Permit Type

| Permit Type | Typical Timeline | County Variance |
|-------------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Building Permit (Spec) | 2-4 weeks from application | Marion faster, Citrus slower due to waivers |
| Building Permit (Custom) | 4-8 weeks | Highly variable based on complexity |
| Septic Permit | 1-2 weeks | Marion: DOH inspection required |
| Demolition Permit | 1-2 weeks | Required BEFORE construction permit |
| Permit Extension | 1-2 weeks | Must request before expiration |
| Permit Modification | 2-4 weeks | May trigger full re-review |

**Permit Validity:** Generally 6 months (except complex projects like condominiums: 1 year). Expiration date resets automatically with each inspection scheduled. If expired 2+ times: risk of complete cancellation.

### Common Rejection Reasons and Resubmission Workflow

From Batch 3 validation interviews, the most common rejection reasons by county:

**Marion County:**
- Missing or outdated survey (survey must be recent)
- Application form incomplete (missing parcel ID or owner information)
- Impact fee documentation missing

**Citrus County:**
- Waiver required but not submitted (~40% of houses need height waiver)
- Arrows documentation missing (drainage signage requirements)
- Civic Application incomplete

**General Rejection Reasons (all counties):**
- Floor plan version mismatch (outdated version submitted)
- Energy calculations not matching current floor plan
- Septic document missing DOH approval
- Missing product approval list

**Resubmission Workflow:**
1. County returns denial with specific notes (reason, reviewer, date)
2. Kaio records denial reason in permit tracking (status: denied/red)
3. Identify corrective action: request updated document from sub, fix application, or prepare waiver
4. Resubmit corrected documentation to county
5. Track resubmission date and monitor for re-review
6. Notes organized by date and author, transfer from application to revision tracking

**Financial Integration:**
- "Ready to issue" status triggers notification to Financeiro (Cintia)
- Financeiro accesses county portal to make permit payment
- Builders Risk can ONLY be filed after Financeiro confirms payment
- System should alert if anyone tries to advance Builders Risk without confirmed payment

## CPM Construction Sequence -- Permits Touchpoints

### Permit Position in CPM Sequence
- **Marion County**: Permit NOT required before starting site work (can proceed until Form)
- **Citrus County**: Permit MUST be issued before any construction begins
- **Permit expiration**: 180 days without inspection activity (Florida Section 553.79(14))

### Pre-Permit Documents Required by CPM
1. Survey (topographic) -- from surveyor
2. Floor Plan -- from Design Office
3. Energy Calculations -- from sub (Quick eCalcs)
4. NOC (Notice of Commencement) -- filed by Lakeshore
5. Septic Plan -- from Rapid Septic Consulting
6. Truss Engineering -- from truss manufacturer

### Inspections Triggered by Permit (CPM Sequence)
All 29 inspection types in the CPM require an active permit:
- UP Inspection, Slab Inspection, Lintel Inspection (Citrus only)
- Framing Inspection, Dry-In Inspection, Rough inspections (Plumbing, AC, Electric)
- Insulation Inspection, Final inspections (Plumbing, Electric, AC, Roof)
- Building Final Inspection -> CO
