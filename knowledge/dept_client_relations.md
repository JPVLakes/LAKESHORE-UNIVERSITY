## Processos

### Processo 1: Atendimento ao Cliente (Dia a Dia)

- **Objetivo:** Responder duvidas, informar status e gerenciar comunicacao com clientes em todas as fases do projeto
- **Responsavel principal:** Juliana Theodoro / equipe
- **Frequencia:** Diario, continuo
- **Ferramentas:** HubSpot (WhatsApp, ligacoes, e-mail integrados), Builder Trend (Portal do Cliente), Daily Log, Planilha de contrato

**Passo a passo:**
1. Receber contato de clientes via WhatsApp (HubSpot), ligacoes e e-mail -- Responsavel: equipe CR -- Ferramenta: HubSpot
2. Identificar tipo de demanda: status de obra, duvida de contrato, solicitacao de upgrade, etc. -- Responsavel: equipe CR -- Ferramenta: HubSpot
3. Buscar informacoes pelo Daily Log -- Responsavel: equipe CR -- Ferramenta: Builder Trend (Daily Log)
4. Quando Daily Log insuficiente: consultar planilha de contrato ou ata -- Responsavel: equipe CR -- Ferramenta: Planilha de contrato
5. Se informacao ainda nao encontrada: consultar time de Schedule ou supervisores -- Responsavel: equipe CR -- Ferramenta: Slack/WhatsApp
6. Comunicar resposta ao cliente -- Responsavel: equipe CR -- Ferramenta: HubSpot
7. Receber demandas de outros setores via canal RCRC (assinatura de documentos, duvidas de contrato, etc.) -- Responsavel: equipe CR -- Ferramenta: Canal RCRC

**Inputs:** Contato do cliente (pergunta, reclamacao, solicitacao), demandas de outros setores via RCRC
**Outputs:** Resposta ao cliente, encaminhamento de demandas
**Observacoes:** A equipe interage com TODOS os setores da empresa. Atualmente so Juliana tem telefone disponivel no HubSpot (limitacao de usuarios). HubSpot ainda em fase de implementacao.

### Processo 2: Gestao de Upgrades (Alteracoes Solicitadas pelo Cliente)

- **Objetivo:** Processar solicitacoes de alteracoes no projeto/construcao feitas pelo cliente
- **Responsavel principal:** Juliana Theodoro / equipe
- **Frequencia:** Conforme demanda do cliente
- **Ferramentas:** HubSpot (WhatsApp), E-mail, Daily Log (Builder Trend)

**Passo a passo:**
1. Cliente solicita upgrade pelo WhatsApp -- Responsavel: equipe CR -- Ferramenta: HubSpot
2. Pedir para cliente formalizar por e-mail -- Responsavel: equipe CR -- Ferramenta: HubSpot
3. Encaminhar fluxo interno por e-mail para cotacao com setores responsaveis -- Responsavel: equipe CR -- Ferramenta: E-mail
4. Receber cotacao de volta dos setores -- Responsavel: equipe CR -- Ferramenta: E-mail
5. Retornar ao cliente com valores -- Responsavel: equipe CR -- Ferramenta: HubSpot
6. Se aprovado: registrar no Daily Log em card fixo -- Responsavel: equipe CR -- Ferramenta: Builder Trend (Daily Log)
7. Acompanhar execucao do upgrade -- Responsavel: equipe CR -- Ferramenta: Builder Trend (Daily Log)

**Inputs:** Solicitacao do cliente, cotacao dos setores responsaveis
**Outputs:** Upgrade aprovado, registrado e acompanhado
**Observacoes:** Tipos comuns: pintura (paleta diferente), ventilador de teto, maquina lavar/secar, armario extra, patio externo. ### Contrato de Construcao — Secoes Relevantes para Client Relations

Principais clausulas do Construction Contract (Lakeshore Lira Investments Corp.) com impacto direto no relacionamento com o cliente:

**Documentos do Contrato (Clause 2)**
- Inclui plantas, especificacoes, Term Sheet e Builder's Draw Schedule
- Materiais indisponiveis podem ser substituidos por equivalentes ou superiores, com autorizacao do cliente
- Especificacoes escritas prevalecem sobre plantas em caso de conflito
- Allowances nao utilizados NAO sao reembolsados

**Preco e Pagamentos (Clause 3)**
- Preco total estimado no contrato (pode variar)
- Progress Payments conforme Builder's Draw Schedule
- Change orders pagos integralmente com o proximo pagamento mensal
- Se custo final for menor que estimado: credito ao cliente em ate 10 dias apos conclusao

**Prazo de Conclusao (Clause 4)**
- Prazo: Certificate of Occupancy dentro de 1 ano apos emissao do building permit e concretagem da laje
- Atrasos por forca maior (desastres naturais, greves, indisponibilidade de materiais, etc.) sao excusaveis

**Change Orders (Clause 5)**
- Devem ser documentos escritos assinados por ambas as partes
- Alteracoes orais sao invalidas e inexecutaveis
- Builder pode exigir pagamento antecipado de change orders adicionais

**Entrega e Pagamento Final (Clause 6)**
- Builder notifica cliente quando construcao esta concluida
- Vistoria conjunta — eventuais deficiencias documentadas em Punch List escrita
- Apos conclusao do Punch List: cliente assina documento de aceite
- Pagamento final exigido apos: (i) conclusao do Work, (ii) emissao do CO, (iii) recebimento do Contractor's Final Affidavit (Fla. Stat. §713.06)

**Garantia do Builder (Clause 13)**
- 1 ano a partir da emissao do CO: garantia contra defeitos de materiais e mao de obra
- Garantia transferivel a terceiros
- Demais garantias implicitas sao expressamente excluidas

**Rescisao pelo Cliente (Clause 11.2)**
- Por inadimplencia do builder (7 dias de aviso): cliente recupera valores nao utilizados e pode tomar posse da propriedade e materiais pagos
- Por falta de financiamento (45 dias do contrato): valores nao utilizados retornam ao cliente; custos de alteracoes de plantas, selecoes e reunioes nao sao reembolsaveis

**Resolucao de Disputas (Clause 15)**
- Sequencia: negociacao direta → mediacao (AAA) → arbitragem vinculante no condado da obra
- Parte vencedora recupera honorarios advocaticios, custos e taxas

## Operational Detail (from Interviews)

### Hold Types

Construction projects can be paused ("held") for different reasons. Each hold type has a distinct cause, resolution process, and escalation chain:

#### Client Hold
- **Cause**: Client-initiated pause -- financing issue, design change request, personal circumstances, or cost dispute
- **Resolution path**: Client meeting with Client Relations team (Juliana) -> updated contract amendment if needed -> client signs approval to resume -> notify Scheduling to restart
- **Escalation chain**: Client Relations (Juliana) -> Sales Manager -> Coordenador (Samuel Torres)
- **Common triggers**: Lender delays draw release, client requests design changes mid-construction, client disputes cost of upgrades exceeding $1,000 threshold

#### Permit Hold
- **Cause**: Waiting for county approval -- rejected documentation, missing documents, county review backlog, waiver requirements (especially Citrus County)
- **Resolution path**: Follow up with county portal and contacts -> provide additional documentation or corrections -> resubmit -> monitor portal daily for resolution
- **Escalation chain**: Permits department (Kaio/Cassia) -> county contacts -> Coordenador if delays exceed 2 weeks
- **Common triggers**: Waiver required for lot elevation (~40% of Citrus County houses), arrow/drainage corrections, survey revisions, floor plan modifications

#### Financial Hold
- **Cause**: Payment issue -- draw not released by lender, client payment overdue, budget overrun requiring approval
- **Resolution path**: Escalate to Financeiro (Cinthia for accounts receivable) -> contact lender directly -> if client-side, Client Relations communicates urgency -> hold released when payment clears
- **Escalation chain**: Financeiro (Cinthia) -> Coordenador (Samuel) -> C-Level (Leo) for payment authorization
- **Common triggers**: Lender processing delays, client cash flow issues, partial payments not supported by system, impact fee payment delays blocking Final Electric inspection

#### Technical Hold
- **Cause**: Engineering or structural issue -- failed inspection, design revision needed, structural calculation error, incompatible materials discovered
- **Resolution path**: Engineering review of the issue -> revised plans or calculations submitted -> county re-review -> resubmit for re-inspection
- **Escalation chain**: Supervisao (field supervisors) -> Engineering firm -> Permits (for revised documents) -> Inspecao (for re-inspection scheduling)
- **Common triggers**: Roof Assembly inspection failure (43% fail rate), framing inspection issues, septic DOH failures, load calculation errors

### Resolution Paths Summary

Each hold type follows a different escalation chain to ensure the right people are involved:

| Hold Type | First Contact | Second Level | Final Escalation |
|-----------|--------------|--------------|-----------------|
| Client Hold | Client Relations (Juliana) | Sales Manager | Coordenador (Samuel) |
| Permit Hold | Permits (Kaio/Cassia) | County contacts | Coordenador |
| Financial Hold | Financeiro (Cinthia) | Coordenador (Samuel) | C-Level (Leo) |
| Technical Hold | Supervisao (field) | Engineering firm | Permits + Inspecao |

## Key Glossary Terms

> Terms from [Glossario de Termos](03_GLOSSARIO.md) most relevant to the Client Relations department.

- **Allowance** / **Provisao/Verba Estimada**: Contractual margin for items with unknown exact cost -- defined with Camila before contract signing
- **Change Order** / **Ordem de Alteracao**: Formal scope/cost/schedule modification after contract -- must be communicated to client
- **Upgrade** / **Melhoria/Adicional**: Client-requested enhancement beyond standard specs with additional cost (e.g., Summer Kitchen, pool)
- **Walk-through** / **Vistoria/Percurso de Inspecao**: Detailed house inspection to identify pending items before delivery to buyer
- **Punch List** / **Lista de Pendencias**: Items needing correction before house delivery -- identified during walkthrough
- **Closing** / **Fechamento/Entrega**: Final house delivery process including title transfer, documents, and account transfers
- **Certificate of Occupancy (CEO/CO)** / **Habite-se**: County certificate confirming house is fit for occupancy -- required before closing
- **Client Hold** / **Paralisacao por Conta do Cliente**: Work stoppage notified to client due to extra cost identified during construction
- **Custom Home** / **Casa Personalizada**: Unique house project per client specifications -- different workflow from spec homes
- **Spec Home** / **Casa Padronizada**: Standard model house from Lakeshore portfolio -- replicable, simpler budget/scheduling
- **By Owner (B/O)** / **Por Conta do Proprietario**: Item that is the client's responsibility, not budgeted by Lakeshore (e.g., pool)
- **Card Fixo** / **Nota Fixa**: Fixed note at top of project in BuilderTrend with critical customization info and special observations

## CPM Construction Sequence -- Client Relations Touchpoints

### Exceeding Costs Sub-System -- Client Hold Path
When costs exceed budget, Client Relations manages the client communication:
1. **Client Hold detected**: CR receives notification of budget overage
2. **Cost evaluation**: Finance determines if cost is estimable
3. **Client communication**: CR presents cost increase to client with documentation
4. **Cost Approved**: Client accepts -> construction continues
5. **Cost Disapproved**: Client rejects -> negotiate alternatives or project discontinuation
